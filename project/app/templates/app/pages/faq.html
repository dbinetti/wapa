Frequently Asked Questions

Who are you?

We're a group of West Ada School District parents who want to support our District in created the best possible educational experience for our students. The site was created by David Binetti, a parent in the West Ada School District.

How is the WAPA different from the PTO?

While both the WAPA and PTO seek to serve our students, the WAPA plays a more active role in policy development and advocacy.

Who pays for this?

Currently we are entirely volunteer-operated and are entirely free to join.

What are you doing with my personal information?

Nothing! Everything stays private unless you wish to make your information public.

What if I have a question you haven't addressed?

Please let us know at support@smilewestada.com so that we can add it to the FAQ!
