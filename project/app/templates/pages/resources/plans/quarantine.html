WASD Quarantine Release Process

You can read the full WASD Safe Return to In-Person Instruction Plan for yourself, but I’ve produced a quick flow chart of the quarantine process below which makes it easier to follow:

Basically, the left side of the flow chart is geared towards those who are vaccinated, follow the mandate, or can provide proof of a prior recovery. The right side is for students who opt-out. And while the process is complicated, the bottom line is: if you follow the mandate, you stay in school. Opt-out of the mandate, and you stay home for ten days and skip sports when exposed — and everyone will be exposed eventually, many times this year.

As you know, the West Ada Parents Association saw this coming and proposed a True North Plan to avoid this “back-door mandate”. Still, the Board decided otherwise, and that’s why the more than 12,000 parents who filled out the opt-out form, drove the district office on short notice, waited in line, and submitted their form with identification in-person are going to be very surprised when they learn their kids are still going to miss school and sports unless they give up their Parental Choice.

In short, this is a deliberate policy of segregation by the West Ada School District, and it must change immediately. If you haven't already done so, please join us and support full and equal access to education for all students.
